Our Mission

We aim to eliminate the chaos of campus breaks. By digitizing the ordering process, we reduce crowding, improve canteen efficiency, and ensure students spend their break time relaxing, not standing in line.

How It Works

1. Browse & Order

Select your favorite canteen shop, browse the live menu, and add items to your cart.

2. Secure Payment

Pay via UPI or Card. Our system securely splits the payment: the shop owner receives their share instantly, and platform fees are processed automatically.

3. Pick Up

Get notified when your food is ready. Show your digital token at the express counter and collect your meal.

Pricing & Commissions

We believe in transparency. NoQ operates on a small commission model per order to maintain platform servers and payment gateway fees. Specific rates are detailed in the Merchant Agreement during onboarding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my payment secure?

Yes. We use banking-grade encryption via licensed payment gateways. We do not store card details.

How do refunds work?

If an order is cancelled by the shop, a refund is automatically initiated to your original payment source within 5-7 business days.

I am a shop owner. How do I join?

Please contact our support team or visit the "Developers" page to reach out for onboarding.
