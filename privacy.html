Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At NoQ, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our platform for food ordering and campus services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Institution / Campus details

Usage Information:

  • Order history
  • Pages visited and activity logs
  • Device and browser information

Payment Information:

We do not store card or UPI details. All payments are securely processed through Razorpay or Cashfree.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your NoQ account
  • Process orders and payments
  • Enable split payments to merchants
  • Improve platform performance
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraud

3. Sharing of Information

We share limited information with:

  • Partner merchants to fulfill your orders
  • Payment gateways for secure processing
  • Technical service providers (hosting, analytics)

We never sell your data.

4. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and industry-standard protection mechanisms to safeguard user data.

5. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account
  • Update your information

Email us at noqeatssrm@gmail.com

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy whenever necessary. Updated versions will be posted on this page.
